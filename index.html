Let Me Tell You About Myself

I've been coding ever since 8 years ago when I started my journey as a front-end developer. My father asked me to build him a website and blog so I used the technologies at the time that were at my disposal. I went in blind with JQuery and JavaScript, HTML,5, and CSS3. I was instantly hooked. I never stopped thinking about new ideas.

I am now a well seasoned developer with technologies at my disposal such as NodeJS, ReactJS, MongoDB, and all the modules that go with them and not to mention automation tools such as Grunt/Gulp and debugging tools like Mocha and Chai and the like.

I am always up-to-date on the latest trends and, most importantly, I never stop learning. This is attested by the fact that my buddies and I always come up with an idea for a new app and give each other one month to build it and present it to ourselves. We essentially learn new ways to develop from each other.

Because of the constant learning and developing I do, I am consistently becoming a better developer day by day and there is no end in sight.
